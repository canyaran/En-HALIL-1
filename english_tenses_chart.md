## Quick Reference Notes:

### When verbs change:
- **Present Simple**: Add **-s** or **-es** for he/she/it (go â†’ goes)
- **"Have to" and "Need to"**: Change to **has to** and **needs to** for he/she/it

### When auxiliaries change:
- **Am/Is/Are**: I am, you/we/they are, he/she/it is
- **Was/Were**: I/he/she/it was, you/we/they were
- **Have/Has**: I/you/we/they have, he/she/it has

### Verb forms used:
- **Base form**: go (used with modals and present simple)
- **Past simple**: went
- **Past participle**: gone (used with perfect tenses)
- **Present participle**: going (used with continuous tenses)
